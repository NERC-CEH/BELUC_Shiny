About this Shiny App

This Shiny app provides a way of exploring and evaluating the Bayesian approach to estimating land use change (BELUC), as published by Levy, et al. (2018). The main purpose is to list assumptions and parameter settings, and show model output summaries based on the choices of parameter and dataset.

The outputs here have been pre-computed, hence the results are restricted to adjusting one variable at a time.
